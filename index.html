About Me

I'm a software engineer driven by passion for programming and constant curiosity to learn. Beyond coding, I enjoy the challenge of strategy games. Furthermore, I like to experiment in the kitchen and try out new recipes. When I'm not coding or cooking, I enjoy spending time with friends and family.

My technical path started at HTL-Braunau, where I focused on Electronics with a specialization on Mobile Computing & Software Engineering. This sparked my interest and led me to pursue a Computer Science Bachelor's degree at JKU. While studying, I enjoyed sharing some knowledge by working as a tutor for the Institute of System Software (SSW) for six semesters.

Somewhat recently, I finished my Master's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Software Engineering at JKU. During my studies I had the opportunity to work as a part-time Software Engineer at Dynatrace, where I contributed to the OneAgent Node.js team. My Master's thesis, "A Generic Machine Code Instrumentation Library", is a challenging project I'm undertaking in collaboration with both the SSW and Dynatrace.

With the educational steps done, I continue to work in the OneAgent Node.js team at Dynatrace in a full-time position. My focus is on enhancing the Node.js OneAgent by adding new features, as well as improving and maintaing the existing agent.
